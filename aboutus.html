October, 2013

How We Met

At the time I was living in a downtown apartment working for a parking company, Francesca was attending Langara College. We happen to go to the same bar that Saturday night and I knew right away, she was special. Everything about her filled me with joy; the way she laughed, the jokes she told, and the happiness she brought wherever she went.

During this time, I was dealing with the ever worsening condition of my father’s health. He had been diagnosed with stage 4 lung cancer; things were not looking good. She had stayed by my side though everything and kept me company even in the hospital when he passed. From then on, I knew, I wasn’t going to let her go anytime soon.

May, 2014

Better Times

We spent time together whenever we could, doing whatever worked to keep me distracted from what had happened.

It was at the point that we both fell in love with going on adventures together.

June, 2015

Trip of a Lifetime

Francesca and I went to Mexico. There, we decided that we wanted to show others the beauty we have found in the world through our own point of view.

December, 2016

Present
